Overview

FashionFinder is an e-commerce concept that curates contemporary clothing and accessories for style-conscious customers. This site plan outlines the project goals, audience, content strategy, visual identity, typography, navigation and wireframes.

Site Purpose

Provide a clean, fast shopping experience for discovering seasonal fashion, with product detail pages, a streamlined checkout, and user accounts for order tracking and personalization.

Target Audience

  • Primary: Adults 18–35, urban, value style and convenience, shop by trends and curated collections.
  • Secondary: Gift shoppers and occasional buyers looking for quality, curated selections.

Key User Scenarios

  1. Discover & buy: "I want a weekend outfit under $80 — where do I browse and check out quickly?"
  2. Gift search: "I need a birthday gift for a friend who loves minimalist style — what suggestions are there?"

Content Outline

  • Homepage: hero, featured collections, trending products, newsletter signup
  • Category pages: filters (size, color, price), sort, product grid
  • Product page: images, description, size guide, reviews, related products
  • Cart & Checkout: editable cart, guest checkout, order summary, payment
  • Account pages: login/register, order history, saved items
  • Support: FAQs, contact form, returns & shipping policies

Testing & Accessibility Checklist

  • Semantic headings and landmarks (one <main>, clear sections)
  • Alt text for all images (logo, products, wireframes)
  • Color contrast: ensure primary/secondary text meets AA (test CTA on background)
  • Keyboard navigation: forms and interactive controls accessible
  • Meta description and language declared
  • Performance: optimize images, preload important fonts, minify CSS for production
